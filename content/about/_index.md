# About Cringeworthy

Cringeworthy is a satirical content platform exploring the darker side of internet culture through a lens of humor and horror.

## Our Mission

We aim to document the most bizarre, unsettling, and genuinely cringeworthy moments in a way that entertains while making you question what you're even doing here.

## Disclaimer

All content on this site is purely satirical and not meant to be taken seriously.
